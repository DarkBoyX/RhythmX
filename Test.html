How RhythmX works
A quick walkthrough — swipe through the steps.
1
Open the Studio. Tap your Profile (top right), then tap Studio inside your profile.
2
Play the on-screen piano. On a phone, tap or slide your finger across the keys. On desktop, click the keys or use your keyboard — white keys are A S D F G H J, black keys are W E T Y U, and Q / Z shift the octave up or down.
3
Record a melody. Press the red Record button, play some notes on the piano, then press it again to stop. Your notes appear in the strip above the piano.
4
Optionally build a beat with the Sequencer. Tap an empty step, then play a piano key to assign that note to the step. Tap ▶ Play to loop it, and adjust BPM for speed.
5
Publish your track. Tap ↑ Publish, give it a title and your name, then hit Publish. It'll show up at the top of the Feed.
6
Discover & support others. On the Feed tab, tap ▶ on a track's waveform to listen. Tap the heart to like a track, and the speech-bubble to comment.
